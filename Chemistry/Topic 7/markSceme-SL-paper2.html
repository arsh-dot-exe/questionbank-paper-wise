CH4 (g) + H2O (g) ⇌ CO (g) + 3H2 (g)
CO (g) + 2H2 (g) ⇌ CH3OH (l)

Consider the first stage of the reaction.

CH4 (g) + H2O (g) ⇌ CO (g) + 3H2 (g)
